Presto Support

Have a question about Presto?

Several members of Facebook's Infrastructure Engineering team work full-time on Presto and they are often around and available for questions. If you have a question about installing, configuring, or using Presto use one of the following resources to find an answer.

Stack Overflow

Stack Overflow is a popular place to ask questions. Read through questions tagged with prestodb or ask your own! You can also search Stack Overflow for "Facebook Presto".

Google Groups mailing list

Search or join the presto-users Google Group to look for questions and answers about installing, configuring, and using Presto.

Facebook Group

As Presto is a Facebook Open Source project, you can join the Facebook Presto Group on Facebook.

IRC

Join the #prestodb room on Freenode.net's IRC network. Most of the Presto contributors will be there.

Twitter

Search for the #prestodb hash tag on Twitter to keep up with the latest React news.
